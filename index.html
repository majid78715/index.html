What is YourCompany AI?

YourCompany AI is an intelligent AI assistant like ChatGPT with the added benefit of being able to train it on your business, your team, your processes, and your clients with your own knowledge base. Use YourCompany AI as an employee to support your team, answer questions, help with creative work, troubleshoot issues, and brainstorm ideas.

What can YourCompany AI do?

Instant Answers To Your Business Questions

No need to track down manuals or sift through endless documents. YourCompany AI analyzes all accumulated documents and acts as an expert on all your company processes to provide the answer you need in seconds.

Upload Any Data & Build Your Knowledge Base

Securely upload anything from PowerPoints to PDFs or crawl an entire website to instantly integrate information and customize YourCompany AI for your company. It will search your database and use it to craft intelligent responses.

Provide Sources For Every Answer

For added peace of mind, you can check YourCompany AI's work. Whether it's from one source or multiple sources, it provides the sources used to come up with every answer.

Boost Employee Efficiency

With instant answers, ideas, and ready-made work, your team can work smarter, not harder. Use YourCompany AI to complete tasks faster, like writing professional emails, translating documents, or creating marketing materials.

Provide Support & Troubleshoot Issues

Not making full use of your digital tools? Can't get the sound to work on Zoom? YourCompany AI uses support documentation to help your team quickly troubleshoot any issues and discover hidden features.

Brainstorm Ideas & Give Suggestions

If you're not sure where to start, you can use YourCompany AI to bring new perspectives and insights to the table. Because it remembers conversations and contextual history, it can give ideas and advice.
