Frequently Asked Questions

How long will it take for the product to arrive?

Products are typically delivered within 1-3 business days after your subscription is approved. For popular items that are in stock, same-day or next-day delivery may be available in select areas.

What payment methods can I use?

We accept all major credit and debit cards, Apple Pay, Google Pay, and bank transfers. You can also pay using installment options through Tabby and Tamara at checkout.

Can I cancel my subscription?

Yes, you can cancel your subscription at any time with no penalties. Simply log into your account, go to your subscription section, and follow the cancellation process. Return shipping is free.

If I want to trade in a product, how do I send it?

When you select the trade-in option during subscription, we'll provide you with a free shipping label. Pack your device securely, attach the label, and drop it off at any approved shipping location. Once we receive and verify the device, your discount will be applied to your next billing cycle.

What happens if my device gets damaged?

With Soum Care included in your subscription, you're fully covered for accidental damage and malfunctions. Simply report the issue through your account, and we'll arrange for repair or replacement at no additional cost.
